About Us

Built for curious technologists.

At EPOCHsociety, we are passionate about technology and its impact on society. We organize workshops, hackathons, and tech talks that cover a wide range of topics from software development to emerging technologies.

Our goal is to foster a vibrant community that encourages learning, creativity, and collaboration among tech enthusiasts.
